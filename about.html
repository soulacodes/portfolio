About Me

💼 My name is Ann Marie Kernen, and I'm currently a Product Designer at Freddie Mac on the myOptigo platform. Before this I was at Wells Fargo on Small Business Banking Technology, and before that a boutique design agency called Futuresight. At all three, I've mainly worked on B2B and enterprise web applications.

📍 Originally hail from the Philly area — fun fact: if you've ever seen Mare of Easttown, that was filmed in my hometown! But I moved to DC for college, fell in love with the city and been here ever since.

💬 I've been passionate about digital design ever since I was a kid who spent hours designing her Myspace profile... then Tumblr after that... Apparently there's an entire generation of us.

💜 And if you've made it this far — thank you for checking out my portfolio!
